# Asset Management

TWY's digital assets — photo shoots, retreat media, brand materials, and marketing content — have been organized from a large mixed archive into a clean, browsable structure.

## How Assets Are Organized

### Active (Frequently Used)

- **Photo Shoots** — 2020 outdoor session and 2022 studio session (over 1,200 images)
- **Retreats** — 8 retreat collections covering Mexico, Joseph OR, Kripalu, and Palouse
- **Brand Assets** — logos, fonts, and brand materials
- **Marketing** — approved ads, email newsletter images, HeyMarvelous thumbnails, and Etifanies social media videos

### Archive (Reference)

- Older photo shoots, retreat media, teacher training materials, and historical photos

Class recordings are stored separately and managed through the clips and class plans systems.

## Pose Detection (Coming Soon)

AI-powered yoga pose detection is being developed to help find specific poses (e.g., "Warrior II") across the photo library and within video recordings. This will make it easier to find the right image for social media, newsletters, or marketing without manually browsing thousands of photos.
